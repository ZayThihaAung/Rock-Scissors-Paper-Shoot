Most people know what's the rock paper scissors AKA rock paper scissors shoot game is. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, paper beats rock. In this game, You have to play till you win 5 times per round. If the computer wins 5 times it wins the a round. If both of you don't win, tie. If you win 3 or 4 rounds you win. Otherwise, computer wins. If both don't win till 5th rounds game is over. In addition, If you're using PC you can play by pressing r for Rock, p for paper, s for scissors and a for autoplay. Press y for yes, n for no and for read rules.
